About project

Solopath is a self-discovery platform for users who want to find themselves and understand themselves better through our carefully curated set of 10 questions.
Our mission is to help people get to know themselves better, understand their needs, and discover what truly matters in their lives. We believe that self-awareness is key to a fulfilling life, and our platform is designed to guide you on this journey.
Based on analyzing over 100+ answered questions, we have selected the 10 best questions that provide quality answers from different perspectives of your life. These questions are designed to provoke thought, introspection, and meaningful self-reflection, ensuring they make a significant impact on you after you answer them.
